Yonkers (/ˈjɒŋkərz/) is a city in Westchester County, New York, and a suburb of New York City. Developed along the Hudson River, it is the third most populous city, after New York City and Buffalo, and ninth most-populous municipality in the state of New York. The population of Yonkers was 211,569 as enumerated in the 2020 United States Census.
